Who We Are

Trusted Partner in Infrastructure & Real Estate Growth

EFAM Services (ETG) is a service-based company headquartered in Juba, South Sudan, specializing in construction, consultancy, real estate, and general supply. We blend local expertise with professional standards to deliver high-quality, affordable solutions for residential, commercial, and institutional projects.

With a client-first approach, we ensure reliable execution, transparent processes, and cost-effective outcomes. Our commitment to excellence drives us to exceed expectations on every project, from design to completion.
